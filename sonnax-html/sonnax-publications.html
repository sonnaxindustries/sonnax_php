Glossary of Sonnax Publications:

Transmission Specialties® Technical Bulletins

Sonnax publishes a quarterly Technical Bulletin for transmission rebuilders featuring technical tips and new products.

TASC Force™ Technical Tips

Monthly technical articles written by members of the Sonnax TASC Force™ are published in Transmission Digest  magazine.

Transmission Specialties® Technical Articles

All the technical articles included in present and past Transmission Specialties® catalogs help to make our catalogs a valuable shop resource for years.

Torque Converter Journals

Quarterly newsletters offer torque converter builders technical and product specific information.

Torque Converter Technical Tips

These articles, published in Transmission Digest magazine, provide advice and solutions for common problems facing torque converter rebuilders

Torque Converter Technical Articles

These technical articles from Sonnax Torque Converter catalogs offer general troubleshooting tips and specific application and product information.
