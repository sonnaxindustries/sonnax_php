Sonnax - the exclusive worldwide supplier of Power Train Savers Products!

Power Train Savers and Sonnax are happy to announce an expanded partnership that names Sonnax the exclusive, worldwide supplier of all Power Train Saver products effective September 1, 2009.

The popularity of Power Train Savers unique over-torque protection system owes much to the strong support of its many customers. Sonnax looks forward to working closely with current and future PTS customers.
