Sonnax Industries, lnc. is a leading supplier to the automotive aftermarket. We are recognized for designing, manufacturing and distributing transmission, torque converter and high performance parts worldwide. Sonnax is recognized as one of the fastest growing companies in the state of Vermont. The Sonnax name has become synonymous with excellent service, innovative designs and customer dedication.

    Staff Accountant

    Our Accounting department is seeking a Staff Accountant to join our team.

    The Staff Accountant will be responsible for posting daily cash receipts and maintaining the A/R subledger, performing account reconciliations and analyses, proactively maintaining standard material inventory costs, participating in month-end closing procedures and assisting with special projects.

    The successful candidate must be detail oriented and have strong communication, analytical, interpersonal, time management and organizational skills. The ability to interact successfully with co-workers within a team oriented environment is also essential.

    The position requires a minimum of an A.S. in Accounting, 1-3 years of relevant experience, a strong level of proficiency with MS Office and previous experience working with an ERP system.

Be a part of a cutting-edge team focused on developing and delivering the best product in the industry, a team whose imagination, hard work and results are rewarded. With Sonnax you will enjoy outstanding benefits, including tuition reimbursement, 401(K) and much more.
